Our Story

What began as a meeting in a basement in Ireland’s Cork City in 1988 has grown continuously and we now have offices right across Europe, Asia, and Canada. From our bases in Shanghai and Singapore to London, Dublin, and beyond, we are committed to discovering the talent you need.

For sure, we’ve gotten bigger …but a big part of us is still sitting behind that desk in Cork all those years ago, fighting hard on our clients’ behalf to discover the best talent possible. Finding the best person is a good start for any recruitment specialist but we’ve always wanted to do a good bit more than that.

We want you, our clients to know that every choice we make for you is backed up by informed thinking and genuine insight. We want you to be sure that every person we recommend has been carefully picked with your role in mind. Most of all, we want you to know that when it comes to getting the right people onboard, you don’t need to go anywhere else.
