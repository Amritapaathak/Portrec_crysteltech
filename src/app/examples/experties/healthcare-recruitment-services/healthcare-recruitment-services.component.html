Our Footprint in India

We are the first and one of the only recruitment agencies in Ireland that has offices in India, we are better placed therefore to source, recruit and support relocation of the best candidates. Our Compliance Teams

We understand that relocating to a new country is a daunting task. Our dedicated compliance teams work with the candidate every step of the way from English language test preparation, nursing registration and visa application right through to all aspects of relocation. We keep in close contact with our hospitals to ensure they have regular updates of their new nurses’ progression.
